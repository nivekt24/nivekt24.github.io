About Me

I am a self-taught and self-driven web developer. Originally, I graduated from California State University, Fullerton with a degree in Bachelor of Science in Health Science. Although I enjoyed the science classes and having worked/interned little in the healthcare field, I could not see myself working in for that field five years or ten years from now. I did some reflecting and remembered, as a child, I was always observing and trying help my father with computer/appliance repairs. I knew I wanted to follow my father's footsteps, so I decided to work with my father, assisting him in computer services and other repair/installation services such as computer repair, computer networking (i.e. routing and switching), appliance repairs, security camera installation, running electrical wires through walls, and more. A year after of working with my father, I have decided I wanted to do something do with computer science rather than IT. I started learning to code from Udemy online courses, and since then, I have been loving every aspect of it; I try to stay updated with the latest programming language, frameworks, libraries, etc. When I am not programming, I enjoy lifting weights, watching/playing basketball, going on food adventures with family/friends, etc. Here is going to be a list of the technologies skills I have gained so far:

  • PHP/OOP
  • Software: Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, Adobe Photoshop, Visual Studio Code, Git, GitHub, Xampp, Gulp, NPM.
